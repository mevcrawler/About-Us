About Us

Who We Are

We are a team of Swedish Web3 experts driven by a simple mission: to build next-generation trading technologies that redefine what’s possible in decentralized finance.

Founded by seasoned blockchain engineers, algorithmic traders, and fintech architects, our company has grown into one of Sweden’s most innovative forces in the Web3 ecosystem. Our work blends Scandinavian precision, deep technical expertise, and an unwavering commitment to transparency and security.

Our Legacy in Web3

Our roots stretch back to the early days of smart contract development, when decentralized finance was still experimental and tools for institutional traders were nearly nonexistent. We began as a small research collective in Stockholm—three engineers exploring how blockchain could unlock new efficiencies in global markets.

From those early experiments, we expanded into a full-scale engineering group focused on:

  • Advanced smart-contract design
  • On-chain data analysis
  • Secure infrastructure for institutional-grade trading
  • High-frequency and cross-chain automation systems

Our breakthrough came as we recognized a gap in the market: institutions wanted to access arbitrage opportunities in Web3, but available tools lacked speed, accuracy, and reliability.

We decided to fix that.

Our Groundbreaking Arbitrage Trader

After years of research, modeling, and iterative testing, we launched our flagship innovation: the newest generation cross-chain Arbitrage Trader, engineered specifically for institutional clients.

This system has quickly become a benchmark in the sector, offering:

  • Millisecond-level execution speeds
  • Real-time multi-chain monitoring
  • Secure, verifiable smart-contract automation
  • Predictive modeling powered by advanced on-chain analytics
  • Minimal slippage and optimized routing across liquidity pools

Today, institutions rely on our technology to identify and capture arbitrage opportunities with unprecedented precision—whether across AMMs, centralized exchanges, or emerging Web3 liquidity hubs.

Our Philosophy

We believe the future of finance is open, permissionless, and guided by technology that empowers rather than obscures.

Our philosophy is anchored in:

  • Integrity: Transparent systems and auditable code.
  • Innovation: Constant iteration, never settling for “good enough.”
  • Security: Institutional-grade protection in every layer.
  • Collaboration: Building with partners that share our long-term vision for Web3.

Looking Ahead

As the global financial landscape evolves, so do we. Our team continues to push the boundaries of what Web3 systems can achieve, expanding our research into:

  • Automated liquidity strategies

  • Market-making systems for L2s

We are proud to stand at the forefront of decentralized financial engineering—and we’re just getting started.
